## 1. PERFORMANCE REVIEW

The Board convened to assess performance against Q2 2026 targets. EffectiveBuzz continues to demonstrate strong product-market fit (PMF) in the AI-powered outbound prospecting space, operating with capital efficiency metrics and scalable unit economics.

### A. Revenue & Financial Position
*   **Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR):** **$1.248M** (Up from $920K in Q1, representing a **35.6% QoQ growth velocity**).
*   **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV):** **$31,200** estimated average based on $260/mo blended user account contracts and typical retention curves.
*   **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** **$3,428** fully loaded across digital marketing, partner commissions, and founder-assisted sales setups.
*   **LTV-to-CAC Ratio:** **9.1x** (Strong margin buffer, supporting aggressive customer acquisition channels).
*   **Gross Operating Margin:** **81.4%** across service levels.
*   **Net Revenue Retention (NRR):** **108.5%** driven by team seat expansions and secondary mailbox additions.

### B. Growth & Acquisition Velocity
*   **Traffic Volume:** **45,000 unique monthly visitors** (72% inbound organic / blog index SEO, 28% targeted B2B Google Ads).
*   **Self-Serve Signup Rate:** **7.0% visitor-to-signup conversion** yielding **3,150 gross signups** monthly.
*   **SDR Activation (DNS Setup success):** **40% sequence setup success** (equivalent to 1,260 trial activations). 
*   **Sales Pipeline Integration:** **54 outbound demo meetings booked monthly** (representing a healthy 1.7% conversion rate from self-serve signups to enterprise conversations).

### C. Product Operations & COGS
*   **Platform Uptime:** **99.98%** over the current billing cycle.
*   **Average API Latency:** **198ms** on core sequence synthesis database queries.
*   **API Cost Control:** **Dual-Tier Cognitive Router setup** successfully routes **88%** of simple text classification workloads to Gemini Flash, keeping the average cost per million tokens highly controlled (~$0.55 blended).

### D. Team & Capacity
*   **Total Headcount:** **14 Full-Time Employees (FTEs)**.
*   **bandwidth Split:** 6 Engineering & Product, 4 Sales & Marketing, 3 Customer success & Support, 1 Finance/Ops.
*   **Support Caseload:** **180 technical tickets monthly**, primarily spiked by SPF, DKIM, and DMARC DNS verification barriers during initial onboarding (responsible for **42% of customer support overhead**).

### E. Market Positioning
*   The outbound automation market faces changing SPF and DKIM provider requirements (specifically Google and Yahoo's stricter limits on high-volume senders).
*   Clients demand "safer", modular delivery networks (multi-mailbox single-user setups) rather than older high-volume automated domains.
*   Regional markets, specifically the EU (DACH region) and GCC countries, are introducing localized data residency mandates (GDPR/sovereignty guidelines), creating high barriers to entry but offering premium enterprise contract potential.

### A. Strategic Risks (Reviewed by Independent Director & Board Chair)
1.  **Onboarding SPF/DKIM Churn Spike:** Over 40% of newly registered accounts struggle to set up custom server deliverability guidelines. This friction drives up customer support tickets (~42% of total caseload) and contributes to high churn rates before Day-14.
2.  **SaaS Seat Theft (Domain Over-loading):** Multi-tenant accounts bypass our premium multi-seat license configurations by assigning single logins to multiple outbound mailbox profiles.
3.  **Data Sovereignty Blockages:** Approximately **$1.4M in regional GCC and European enterprise pipeline deals** are stalled due to lack of local database replication paths.

### B. Strategic Opportunities (Reviewed by CEO & Lead Investor)
1.  **AI Playground Acquisition Funnel:** Placing a lightweight, anonymous outbound outline generator sandbox on our public landing pages can elevate traffic-to-signup conversion ratios from 7.0% to 11.0%+.
2.  **Indirect Partner Alliances (EMEA/GCC VARs):** Collaborating with systems integrators and local sales consultancies by offering a **30% Year-1 referral commission** allows capital-light distribution into premium markets.
