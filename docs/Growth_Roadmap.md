## 3. The 90-Day Execution Roadmap

### Phase 1: Days 1 - 30 (The Inbound-Outbound Sprint)
- **Primary Channels:** Founder Outbound (email/Loom), LinkedIn organic.
- **Milestone Goal:** 15 paying customers.
- **Actions:**
  1.  **Founder Outbound Boot-up:** Setup 3 cold outreach domains, connect them to the EffectiveBuzz outbound engine, and send 50 highly targeted emails daily using our trigger-event criteria (hiring SDRs, recently funded).
  2.  **LinkedIn Storytelling Loop:** Founder begins posting daily on personal LinkedIn. Themes: Behind-the-scenes building EffectiveBuzz, teardowns of bad cold emails, and sharing live sandbox output screenshots.
  3.  **The Private Launch Group:** Invite 50 people from the waitlist into a "Founding Members" private Slack. Offer a heavily discounted lifetime promo lock-in in exchange for daily product feedback and public reviews.

### Phase 2: Days 31 - 60 (The Launch & Leverage Phase)
- **Primary Channels:** Product Hunt Launch, Agency Partnerships.
- **Milestone Goal:** 50 paying customers (Cumulative).
- **Actions:**
  1.  **Product Hunt Launch (Week 5):** Execute our Product Hunt campaign. Partner with an established top hunter, send waitlist notifications, run a 24-hour social campaign, and offer a special "PH Launch Badge" that includes 1,000 free bonus AI credits.
  2.  **Agency Partner Engine:** SREs and CS reps pitch to high-volume Lead Generation agencies. Offer them white-labeled sandbox portals or custom multi-mailbox dashboards in exchange for bulk student/seat purchases.
  3.  **Referral Engine Boot:** Activate the in-app "Share EffectiveBuzz & get free credits" viral loop.

### Phase 3: Days 61 - 90 (The Organic Scale Engine)
- **Primary Channels:** SEO/Editorial Content, Automated Virality.
- **Milestone Goal:** 100 paying customers (Cumulative).
- **Actions:**
  1.  **SEO Content Engine:** Publish 5 high-converting "Alternative to [Competitor]" vertical landing pages comparing EffectiveBuzz against Clay, Apollo, and Instantly. Target high-buying-intent searches.
  2.  **The "SDR Clinic" Webinar:** Host a bi-weekly, live interactive teardown of guest-submitted campaigns, running them live through the platform. 
  3.  **Active Churn Mitigation:** CSMs actively deploy our account health score routines to lock-in the first 50 accounts while expanding scale pricing models.
