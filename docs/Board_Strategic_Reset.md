## 1. COMPREHENSIVE BOARD REVIEW

The Board of Directors, representing our venture partners and strategic founders, has conducted a thorough operational review of EffectiveBuzz's performance in Q2 2026. This review establishes a baseline for our high-value reset.

### A. Product Status: "The Deliverability & Integration Mandate"
*   **Assessment:** While our generative sequence writing features show solid customer usage (CSAT at 82%), core customer churn is highly correlated with custom domain delivery problems and CRM sync isolation. Simple AI outbound wrappers are getting commoditized.
*   **Directive:** We must pivot the product from simple list automation into an active **Deliverability Shield** and **Enterprise CRM Synchronization Engine**.

### B. Revenue Status: "The Expansion & ARPU Playbook"
*   **Assessment:** Standard list subscriptions ($95/mo Growth, $299/mo Pro) generate a steady $1.248M ARR from 603 accounts. However, large customers share single admin profiles across countless corporate domains, leaving significant revenue unmutilated.
*   **Directive:** Move to value-based seat expansion pricing ($45/mo per tambahan seat) and monetize DNS diagnostics and Deliverability Shields as standard $50/mo add-ons.

### C. Team & Talent Status: "Specialist Delivery & VAR Hiring"
*   **Assessment:** General engineering resources are sufficient, but we lack depth in specialized deliverability engineering and local market sales channels. This creates onboarding bottlenecks and delays global enterprise expansions.
*   **Directive:** Hire a specialized **Deliverability Lead Engineer** immediately and partner with local EMEA **Value-Added Resellers (VAR)** to scale distribution without expanding our salary baseline.

### D. Infrastructure Status: "Global Pod Sovereignty"
*   **Assessment:** 15% of our active pipeline is blocked due to GDPR worries and strict GCC data residency laws requiring local database hosting in physical centers like Moro Hub UAE or AWS Frankfurt.
*   **Directive:** Deploy **AWS local database nodes (Frankfurt eu-central-1)** and **GCC regional cloud nodes** to bypass sovereignty audits and clear enterprise procurement.

### E. AI Systems: "Cognitive Dual-Tier Model Tuning"
*   **Assessment:** Running all customer sequence generation on high-cost reasoning models is unsustainable. We need clear semantic boundaries to prevent margin leakage.
*   **Directive:** Implement an interactive **Dual-Tier LLM Cognitive Router** using Gemini Flash for lightweight categorization, routing heavy sequence synthesis to advanced reasoning models only.
