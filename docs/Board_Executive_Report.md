## SECTION 2 — Top 5 System & Operational Risks

As we scale our operations from $1M to $10M ARR, the ELT has identified the following top 5 risks and established clear mitigation procedures for each:

### 1. Delivery Spam Filters & Domain Warmup Limits (High Risk — COO/CTO)
*   **Risk:** Google and Microsoft are continuously updating their spam classification algorithms. Sudden shifts in daily sending volumes can affect deliverability rates and increase domain blocks.
*   **Mitigation:** We have launched our Pre-Flight Deliverability Shield, which blocks risky emails before delivery, and automated DNS wizards that verify customer SPF, DKIM, and DMARC settings during setup.

### 2. High Model API Platform Dependence (Medium Risk — CPO/CTO)
*   **Risk:** Standard SaaS tools that operate purely as simple LLM wrappers face high operating costs, slow performance, and potential reliability issues from single-supplier APIs.
*   **Mitigation:** We have deployed a dual-tier model routing system. Standard tasks are routed to efficient models, and we use local fallback options to protect our service reliability and gross margins.

### 3. Regulatory Data Sovereignty Rules (Medium Risk — COO/CFO)
*   **Risk:** Expanding into Europe (GDPR) and the Middle East (NDMO/PDPL) introduces strict regulatory requirements. Storing European customer contacts or Middle Eastern government records on US-central nodes is an existential regulatory risk.
*   **Mitigation:** In Q1, we are launching an isolated EU-central (Frankfurt) AWS pod. We are also deploying Moro Hub database nodes in Dubai to support sovereign local hosting.

### 4. Direct Lower-Cost Wrappers (Medium/Low Risk — CRO/CEO)
*   **Risk:** Low-cost developer teams can quickly compile basic prompt-writing systems and offer high-volume outbound lists for low monthly rates, undercutting list pricing.
*   **Mitigation:** We focus on deep, bidirectional workflow integration. By syncing directly with customer CRM platforms like Salesforce and HubSpot, we make it highly complex and costly for enterprises to switch systems.

### 5. Talent Churn in Critical Engineering Roles (Low Risk — CEO/COO)
*   **Risk:** As a venture-backed startup, losing senior cognitive engineering talent can stall our technical roadmap and delay core database product updates.
*   **Mitigation:** We have aligned employee stock options with performance goals, established structured development processes, and expanded our recruiting pipeline to access global engineering hubs.
