# EffectiveBuzz: Expansion Revenue & NRR Roadmap

**Prepared By:** SaaS Revenue Strategist  
**Date:** June 7, 2026  
**Project:** EffectiveBuzz  
**Objective:** Drive Net Revenue Retention (NRR) above 120% by systematically operationalizing seat expansion, consumption-based upselling (AI Credits), and Enterprise tier migrations.

---

## SECTION 1 — Seat Expansion Strategy (The Multi-Player Loop)

Expansion happens when a single-player tool becomes a team-wide operating system.

*   **The "Share the Setup" Trigger:** Getting a domain warmed up, connecting sub-inboxes, and dialing in the AI context takes work. Once an SDR Manager or Founder has it working perfectly, we prompt: *"Your outbound engine is running flawlessly. Invite your reps to leverage your pre-configured AI models and high-reputation sending domains."*
*   **Collaborative Inbox FOMO:** In the Unified Inbox, when an Admin views a hot reply, they see a CTA: `[@ Mention Team Member to Hand-off]`. Clicking this triggers the seat invite modal if they are out of available seats on their current tier.
*   **Leaderboard Mechanics:** Introduce a "Meetings Booked Leaderboard" on the Analytics dashboard. Single-player accounts see: *"Teams using EffectiveBuzz book 3x more meetings. Upgrade to Growth to invite your team and track performance."*
*   **Frictionless Adds:** True-up billing. Let Admins invite users instantly without a credit card block; bill the prorated amount on the next monthly cycle.

---

## SECTION 2 — AI Credit Expansion (The Consumption Engine)

As users find success, their volume naturally increases. We monetize the success of the platform through usage-based pricing.

*   **The 80% Warning Banner:** When a workspace consumes 80% of its monthly AI Credit allocation, a persistent banner appears: *"You're crushing it this month! You have 800 AI credits left. Enable auto-refill to ensure your active campaigns don't pause."*
*   **The Contextual Block:** If a user tries to generate a list of 5,000 AI emails but only has 1,000 credits left, the system blocks the action and offers a 1-click modal: `[ Buy 5,000 Credits for $100 ]` or `[ Upgrade to Scale Tier for 15,000 Monthly Credits ]`.
*   **The ROI Summary Email:** At the end of the month, send an email: *"EffectiveBuzz AI researched 4,500 prospects and booked 12 meetings for you. You used 100% of your credits. Upgrade to the Scale Tier to double your pipeline next month."*

---

## SECTION 3 — Enterprise Upgrade Strategy (The Whale Hunt)

Moving successful Mid-Market accounts on the $899/mo Scale Tier into $35k+/year Enterprise contracts.

*   **The Usage Ceiling Trigger:** When an account hits the ceiling of the Scale Plan (100k active prospects or 5 workspaces), Customer Success gets a Slack alert to manually reach out.
*   **The "Bring Your Own Model" (BYOM) Pitch:** Target large sales orgs with custom LLM fine-tuning. *"We see you're sending high volume. On the Enterprise tier, we can fine-tune our AI model explicitly on your historical closed-won Salesforce data to increase conversion rates by 20%."*
*   **Security & Compliance Triggers:** When a user creates a support ticket asking for SOC2 documentation, SAML/SSO integration, or custom data retention policies, this is an immediate Enterprise routing trigger.
*   **Executive Business Reviews (EBRs):** Quarterly check-ins with Scale accounts. The CSM proves the ROI (e.g., "$5M in pipeline generated") and pitches the Enterprise SLA and dedicated infrastructure.

---

## SECTION 4 — Renewal Playbooks (Defending the Base)

Protecting existing revenue is cheaper than acquiring new revenue.

*   **The 90-Day Warning (Annual Contracts):** 90 days before an annual contract renews, the CSM pulls product telemetry. If the Customer Health Score (CHS) is < 60, the CSM triggers the "At-Risk Renewal" playbook—a mandatory executive alignment call to fix adoption gaps.
*   **The "Annual Flip" Playbook (Monthly Contracts):** For users who have paid successfully for 4 consecutive months on a monthly plan, trigger an automated in-app message and email: *"You've been successfully using EffectiveBuzz for 4 months. Lock in a 20% discount and upgrade to an annual plan today."*
*   **The "Off-boarding Save" Playbook:** When a user clicks "Cancel Subscription", do not just let them go. 
    1. Force a 3-question churn survey.
    2. If the reason is "Too Expensive", offer an immediate 2-month 50% discount to stay.
    3. If the reason is "Temporary Pause", offer a $10/mo "Data Parking" plan that pauses campaigns but saves all CRM and configuration data.
