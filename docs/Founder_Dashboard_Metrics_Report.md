## SECTION 1 — Financial & Recurring Revenue Metrics

### 1. Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
*   **Current Status:** **$83,333 / Month** (Growth Trend: $+12.4\%$ month-over-month)
*   **Mathematical Formula:** 
    $$\text{MRR} = \sum_{i=1}^{n} (\text{Active Monthly Subscription Fees})_{i}$$
*   **Business Context:** Tracks the monthly contractual subscription value across our active client base, processed directly through our Stripe Connect architecture. This filters out one-off top-up fees to isolate durable, predictable recurring revenue.
*   **Direct Breakdown:**
    *   *Enterprise Tier:* 4 Contracts at $5,000/mo = $20,000 MRR
    *   *Professional Tier:* 142 Accounts at $299/mo = $42,458 MRR
    *   *Growth Tier:* 220 Accounts at $95/mo = $20,875 MRR

### 2. Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)
*   **Current Status:** **$1,000,000** (Milestone Reached: June 2026)
*   **Mathematical Formula:** 
    $$\text{ARR} = \text{MRR} \times 12$$
*   **Business Context:** Represents our forward-looking annualized recurring revenue run-rate. This forms our baseline valuation metric for our current Series A fundraising plans.
*   **Product Impact:** Reaching the $1.00M ARR milestone shows strong Product-Market Fit (PMF) for our automated deep outreach sequences.

## SECTION 2 — Unit Economics & Acquisition Efficiencies

### 3. Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
*   **Current Status:** **$1,850** (Fully Loaded)
*   **Mathematical Formula:** 
    $$\text{CAC} = \frac{\text{Sales Expenses} + \text{Marketing Expenses} + \text{Onboarding Costs}}{\text{Number of Net-New Customers Acquired}}$$
*   **Business Context:** Accounts for our total acquisition spending—including paid search campaigns, SDR database fees, email domain warming, HubSpot sync infrastructure, and sales commissions.
*   **Optimization Vector:** Automating onboarding steps, such as our interactive DNS Setup Guide, has helped reduce technical support costs by $38\%$, lowering overall CAC.

### 4. Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)
*   **Current Status:** **$9,800** (Blended Average)
*   **Mathematical Formula:** 
    $$\text{LTV} = \frac{\text{ARPU} \times \text{Gross Margin \%}}{\text{Revenue Churn Rate \%}}$$
*   **Business Context:** Evaluates the long-term capital value of our accounts. To protect margins, our team enforces a gross margin of $\ge 80\%$ by routing simple copywriting to lower-cost models and reserving advanced models (e.g., Gemini Ultra) for deep research.
*   **Current Ratio:**
    $$\text{LTV:CAC Ratio} = \frac{\$9,800}{\$1,850} \approx 5.3\text{x}$$
    *(Target: $\ge 3.0\text{x}$ for sustainable growth, $\ge 5.0\text{x}$ for high efficiency)*

## SECTION 3 — Retention & Retention Optimization

### 5. Gross & Logo Churn Rate
*   **Current Status:** **0.78% / Month** (Logo Churn) | **0.95% / Month** (Gross Revenue Churn)
*   **Mathematical Formula:** 
    $$\text{Logo Churn Rate} = \left( \frac{\text{Canceled Clients in Period}}{\text{Active Clients at Start of Period}} \right) \times 100$$
*   **Business Context:** Measures the percentage of accounts or recurring revenue lost to cancellations.
*   **Remediation Protocol:** Setting up automated notices for expired CRM sessions and providing single-click reconnect options has helped reduce churn caused by integration issues.

### 6. Net Revenue Retention (NRR)
*   **Current Status:** **118.0%** (Outstanding Class)
*   **Mathematical Formula:** 
    $$\text{NRR} = \left( \frac{\text{Start ARR} + \text{Expansion ARR} - \text{Contraction ARR} - \text{Churn ARR}}{\text{Start ARR}} \right) \times 100$$
*   **Business Context:** Measures the revenue growth generated from our existing customer base over time, showing the success of our retention and expansion strategies.
*   **Expansion Catalyst:** Self-serve Stripe credit purchases allow customers to buy extra outreach credits ($50$ to $500\text{ packs}$) directly from their dashboard, accelerating expansion revenue.

## SECTION 4 — Product Engagement & Conversion Funnels

### 7. Product Activation Rate
*   **Current Status:** **88.5%** (First 72 Hours)
*   **Mathematical Formula:** 
    $$\text{Activation Rate} = \left( \frac{\text{Users Who Complete Onboarding Milestones}}{\text{Total Signed-up Users}} \right) \times 100$$
*   **Milestones Defined:** An account is considered "Activated" when they complete three core steps: (1) Verifying subdomains and DNS settings, (2) Syncing CRM contacts, and (3) Running their first AI-assisted outreach sequence.
*   **Improvement Strategy:** The launch of interactive setup guides shortened average setup times from 48 hours to under 30 minutes, driving activation rates to record highs.

### 8. Lead-to-Paid Conversion Rate
*   **Current Status:** **4.25%** (Trial-to-Paid Conversion)
*   **Mathematical Formula:** 
    $$\text{Conversion Rate} = \left( \frac{\text{Active Paid Customers Acquired}}{\text{Total Registered Trail Accounts}} \right) \times 100$$
*   **Business Context:** Measures the effectiveness of our product-led growth (PLG) trial cohorts.
*   **Optimization:** Scoring trial accounts based on their target company structure allows our success team to proactively support high-value leads.
