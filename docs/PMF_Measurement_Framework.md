# EffectiveBuzz: Product-Market Fit (PMF) Measurement Framework

**Prepared By:** Product Strategy Consultant & SaaS Growth Analyst  
**Date:** June 7, 2026  
**Project:** EffectiveBuzz  
**Objective:** Define the exact qualitative and quantitative thresholds required to confidently declare Product-Market Fit before significantly scaling acquisition spend.

---

## SECTION 1 — Activation Metrics (The "Aha" Moment)

Activation measures how quickly and reliably a new user experiences the core value of the platform.

*   **Setup Completion Rate:** % of users who connect a mailbox, upload >10 prospects, and configure a sending profile.
*   **Time-To-Value (TTV):** Median time from account creation to the first AI-generated campaign launch. 
    *   *Target:* < 15 minutes.
*   **First-Value Event Rate:** % of created workspaces that generate at least 5 AI-personalized emails within 24 hours.
*   **Approval Rate:** % of AI-generated drafts approved by the user without manual editing. (Measures AI exactness/trust).

## SECTION 2 — Retention Metrics (The Value Loop)

Retention proves the product solves an ongoing, painful problem and isn't just a novelty.

*   **Weekly Active Senders (WAS):** % of users who have an active, sending campaign running during a 7-day trailing window.
*   **D1 / D7 / D30 Retention:** % of users who log into the application on Day 1, Day 7, and Day 30 following activation.
    *   *Target D30:* > 60%
*   **Unified Inbox Habit:** Average number of incoming replies viewed/handled inside the EffectiveBuzz CRM vs. natively in Gmail/Outlook. (Proves product stickiness).
*   **Logo Churn Rate:** % of paying workspaces that cancel or fail to renew per month.
    *   *Target:* < 3.5% monthly.

## SECTION 3 — Expansion Metrics (The Scale Vector)

Expansion proves that the product becomes deeply embedded in the customer's organizational operations over time.

*   **Seat Expansion Velocity:** Average time from initial single-seat purchase to adding team members (SDRs/Managers).
*   **AI Credit Overage Rate:** % of users on base tiers triggering variable usage billing via Stripe due to high AI volume.
    *   *Target:* 15-20% of the active base hitting overages (indicating healthy power-user growth without forcing total base churn).
*   **Tier Upgrade Rate:** % of Starter ($99) accounts upgrading to Growth ($299) within the first 90 days.

## SECTION 4 — Revenue Metrics (The Business Model)

Ensuring PMF translates to a sustainable, scalable financial construct.

*   **CAC Payback Period:** The number of months required to recoup the Customer Acquisition Cost based on Gross Margin.
    *   *Target:* < 3 months.
*   **LTV:CAC Ratio:** Lifetime value of a customer compared to their blended acquisition cost.
    *   *Target:* > 4:1.
*   **Net Revenue Retention (NRR):** Revenue from existing customers (including expansions/upsells minus churn/downgrades).
    *   *Target:* > 115%.
*   **Hardware/AI Gross Margin:** Revenue per user minus server and Gemini API execution costs.
    *   *Target:* > 85%.

## SECTION 5 — PMF Survey Framework (The Qualitative Signal)

We will trigger the Sean Ellis PMF Survey at Day 45 of a user's lifecycle (after they have completed at least one full outbound sequence cycle and analyzed replies).

*   **The Core Question:** "How would you feel if you could no longer use EffectiveBuzz?"
    *   *A) Very Disappointed* (The PMF Threshold Tracker)
    *   *B) Somewhat Disappointed*
    *   *C) Not Disappointed*
*   **Follow-Up 1:** "What is the primary benefit you have received from EffectiveBuzz?" (Evaluates positioning alignment).
*   **Follow-Up 2:** "What type of person do you think would benefit most from EffectiveBuzz?" (Refines the ICP).
*   **Follow-Up 3:** "How can we improve EffectiveBuzz for you?" (Builds the product roadmap).
*   **Customer Effort Score (CES):** "After your AI generated its first batch of drafts, how easy was it to launch your campaign?" (Rated on a 1-5 Scale).

## SECTION 6 — Customer Segmentation (Cohorted Analysis)

Metrics will be segmented on the PMF Dashboard to avoid blending noisy signals across disparate use cases:

*   **Founders / Early-Stage Startups:** Focus heavily on Activation rates and "Very Disappointed" PMF scores. High churn risk but high velocity.
*   **Lead Gen Agencies:** Focus on Expansion limits (AI Credits, Workspace limits). Extremely high retention and platform volume.
*   **SDR Teams (Mid-Market):** Focus on Seat Expansion and Collaboration metrics (sharing lead scores, inbox collaboration).

## SECTION 7 — Success Thresholds (The Scaling Triggers)

We will **NOT** inject major capital into paid marketing or massive sales hiring until the following thresholds light up *green* on the PMF Dashboard:

1.  **The Sean Ellis Test:** > 40% of surveyed users answer "Very Disappointed" if the product disappeared.
2.  **The Habit Loop:** D30 Retention > 60% for activated users.
3.  **The Economic Engine:** CAC Payback is demonstrably under 3 months via Founder-led and organic channels.
4.  **The AI Trust Factor:** Users are sending > 80% of AI-generated emails without manually overriding or rewriting the generated text.
