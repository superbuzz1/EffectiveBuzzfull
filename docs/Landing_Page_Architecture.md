**Objective:** Convert high-intent B2B visitors into trials and booked demos through conversion-optimized UX and pain-focused copywriting.

---

*   **Headline:** Turn Your Total Addressable Market Into Predictable Pipeline with AI.

*   **Prospecting Pain (The Quality vs. Quantity Trap):** "Volume-based cold email is dead. Your buyers can spot a mail-merge template from a mile away. Blasting thousands of generic emails burns your best leads forever and ruins your domain reputation."

*   **Founder Pain (The Scaling Ceiling):** "Scaling outbound traditionally means adding massive headcount overhead. Skyrocketing Customer Acquisition Costs (CAC) are crushing your margins."

## SECTION 6 — Pricing

**Presentation Strategy:** Anchor high, drive toward the middle tier.
*   **Layout:** 3-Column pricing table (Starter, Growth, Scale) + an Enterprise text block below.
*   **The Anchor:** Highlight the `Growth ($299/mo)` tier visually by popping it out with a subtle glow or banner reading `"Most Popular for Sales Teams"`.
*   **The Value Framing:** Above the pricing grid, add micro-copy: *"Hire an AI-powered SDR for less than the cost of one day of a human rep."*
*   **The Friction Reducer:** A large toggle for `[ Monthly / Annually ]` displaying `"Save 20% / 2 Months Free"` when toggled to Annually.

## SECTION 7 — FAQ

**Top Objections Addressed:**
1.  **Will this hurt my domain reputation and send me to spam?**
    *   *Answer:* "No. EffectiveBuzz integrates natively with Resend and features built-in volume governors, bounce protection, and Spintax AI variability to keep your deliverability pristine."
2.  **Is the AI going to hallucinate and send embarrassing emails?**
    *   *Answer:* "You always have control. You can set campaigns to 'Human-in-the-Loop' mode, meaning the AI drafts the emails, but nothing sends until you click approve."
3.  **How is this different from Apollo or Instantly?**
    *   *Answer:* "Those are volume platforms designed to send the same template to 10,000 people. We are a personalization platform designed to send 10,000 highly unique, deeply researched emails contextually mapped to each buyer."
