## 3. STRATEGIC WEEKLY OPERATING PLAN

### 1. Biggest Problem This Week: "Subdomain Setup Friction"
*   **Analysis:** SPF, DKIM, and DMARC setups are major hurdles for new SME and Mid-Market teams. It represents **42% of support ticket volume**, retarding initial time-to-value (TTV) and lowering potential CSAT scores.
*   **Strategic Resolution:** Build an automated, interactive DNS Diagnostic Health Wizard within the user workspace to eliminate manual validation processes.

### 2. Biggest Opportunity This Week: "The Enterprise Land-and-Expand Seat Loop"
*   **Analysis:** Over **45%** of active Mid-Market administrative profiles are sharing credentials to run multi-domain campaigns, bypassing seat monetization.
*   **Strategic Resolution:** Roll out seat scaling surcharges ($45/mo for each extra mailbox profile) to easily drive an immediate 15% ARR expansion without direct marketing costs.

### 3. Most Important Operational KPI: "SDR Active Setup Velocity"
*   **Primary Focus:** Time-to-First-Outbound-Sequence (TTFOS). Shrinking this setup metric from **5 days down to less than 2 hours** will directly reduce early churn risk during the critical Day-1 to Day-7 cohort.
