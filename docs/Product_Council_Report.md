### TRACK 1: BUILD NOW (Committed / Immediate Execution)
*   **A. Active Pre-Flight Deliverability Shield & DNS diagnostics**
    *   *Problem:* 42% of customer support tickets stem from domain setup confusion. Poor subdomain health triggers early contract terminations.
    *   *Strategic Rationale:* Protects sending authority instantly. Onboarding velocity shrinks from 24 hours to under 2 hours, boosting first-week retention.
    *   *Revenue Impact:* High (Saves existing $120k ARR at risk; drives CSAT lift).
    *   *Engineering Cost:* Low/Medium (Relatively simple DNS validators and warning thresholds).
    *   *Priority Rank:* **1**
*   **B. Dual-Tier LLM Cognitive Router**
    *   *Problem:* Token leakage occurs when repeating generative outlines across deep, high-cost reasoning models.
    *   *Strategic Rationale:* Directs common categorizations and lightweight edits to fast models (Gemini Flash), reserving premium models for sequence synthesis.
    *   *Revenue Impact:* High (Improves blended gross operating margins by +4.2%).
    *   *Engineering Cost:* Medium (Requires prompt classification blocks).
    *   *Priority Rank:* **2**

---

### TRACK 2: BUILD NEXT (High Strategic Value / Q4 Commitment)
*   **A. Bidirectional Salesforce & HubSpot Sync Premium Modules**
    *   *Problem:* Enterprise accounts refuse manual CSV list uploads, demanding automated syncing of leads, sequence states, and active reply scores.
    *   *Strategic Rationale:* Implements a deep switching barrier for accounts. Keeps EffectiveBuzz embedded in daily sales operations.
    *   *Revenue Impact:* High (Essential for capturing larger $50k+ ACV corporate accounts).
    *   *Engineering Cost:* High (Requires oauth handling, sync queues, and multi-tenant schema extensions).
    *   *Priority Rank:* **3**
*   **B. Outbound Mailbox Seat Expansion Surcharges**
    *   *Problem:* Mid-market customer teams share single login credentials across many domains, avoiding seat expansion costs.
    *   *Strategic Rationale:* Limits standard plans to 3 active mailboxes; bills $45/mo for each extra sender profile to create expansion loops.
    *   *Revenue Impact:* Medium/High (Adds immediate expansion MRR from existing cohorts).
    *   *Engineering Cost:* Low (Standard Stripe pricing parameters update).
    *   *Priority Rank:* **4**

---

### TRACK 3: BUILD LATER (Scaling Horizon / 6-12 Month GTM)
*   **A. ISO/IEC 27001 Compliance Protocols & Sovereign Database Pods**
    *   *Problem:* $1.4M in regional DACH enterprise pipelines is stalled due to strict data-sovereignty residency audits.
    *   *Strategic Rationale:* Deploys physically isolated German (AWS Frankfurt) or Middle Eastern (UAE Moro Hub) localized nodes to clear security audits.
    *   *Revenue Impact:* Medium (Clears pending sovereign enterprise contracts with premium surcharges).
    *   *Engineering Cost:* High (Complex multi-region postgres pgvector database clustering).
    *   *Priority Rank:* **5**
*   **B. Unified Certified "Autonomous Revenue Engineer" Course Curriculum**
    *   *Problem:* Direct sales acquisition inside European markets is slow and expensive.
    *   *Strategic Rationale:* Partners with regional RevOps consultants, giving them 30% recurring Year-1 commissions for promoting our certified system.
    *   *Revenue Impact:* Medium (Drives high-velocity capital-efficient customer streams).
    *   *Engineering Cost:* Low (Informational academies and training portals).
    *   *Priority Rank:* **6**

---

### TRACK 4: DO NOT BUILD (Explicitly Eliminated)
*   **A. Custom Outbound B2B Outreach Foundation LLM**
    *   *Reason:* Pre-training base models from scratch is capital intensive and yields no workflow defense advantage. Commercial APIs deprecate faster than any training runway can justify. Keep leveraging third-party APIs.
    *   *Priority Rank:* **ELIMINATED**
*   **B. Multi-Channel B2C Consumer Social Automation**
    *   *Reason:* B2C automation involves severe security risks, platform blockages, high spam rates, and high customer turnover. Stating focused on B2B enterprise SaaS is crucial for long-term health.
    *   *Priority Rank:* **ELIMINATED**
